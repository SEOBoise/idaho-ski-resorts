Privacy Policy for Ski-Resorts-in-Idaho.com

Last Updated: 10/25/2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to Ski-Resorts-in-Idaho.com. We are committed to protecting the personal information and privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to safeguard it.

2. Personal Information We Collect

  • Automatically Collected Data: When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including your IP address, the type of browser you are using, and some of the cookies installed on your device.
  • Directly Provided Data: We may collect information you provide directly to us such as your name, email address, postal address, phone number, and other identification information when you register, subscribe, or communicate with us.

3. How We Use Your Personal Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and feedback.
  • Improve our website and services.
  • Send newsletters, marketing promotions, or other communications you've opted into.
  • Detect, prevent, and handle technical issues.

4. Sharing of Your Personal Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share aggregated, anonymized data that does not identify you with third-party business partners for the purpose of analytics and advertising.

5. Your Rights

Access: You can request details of personal information we hold about you.
Correction: You have the right to correct data that we hold about you that is inaccurate or incomplete.
Erasure: In certain circumstances, you can ask for the data we hold about you to be erased from our records.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your experience. Cookies are files with a small amount of data, often used as unique anonymous identifiers. You have the option to accept or refuse these cookies.

7. Third-party Services

We may use third-party services to support our website operations. These third-party services may collect, store, use, and share data in ways that are detailed in their individual privacy policies.
